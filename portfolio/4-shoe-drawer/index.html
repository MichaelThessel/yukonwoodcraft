Shoe Shelf with hidden Drawer

  • Client Lianne C.

For this project a custom shoe shelf was created that utilizes an existing nook in the wall. The shelf can slide out to reveal a hidden drawer to store additional shoes. The drawer can be resized to account for seasonal footwear size differences.
